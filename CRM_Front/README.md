# Rank Riser - Rate Your Customer Experience

Welcome to **Rank Riser**, a user-friendly web application built with React and Vite, designed to let customers share their experiences by rating services, selecting staff members, and providing feedback. Whether you're praising exceptional service or suggesting improvements, Rank Riser makes it easy to share your thoughts in a fun and engaging way.

## Features

### 1. Rate with Emojis

- **Express Your Mood:** Choose from a set of intuitive emojis (e.g., happy, neutral, sad) to quickly rate your customer experience.
- **Large, Vibrant Emojis:** Easily select your rating with visually appealing, large emojis that make the process enjoyable.
- **Instant Feedback:** Your emoji selection is instantly recorded, reflecting your experience with clarity.

### 2. Select Employees

- **Staff Recognition:** Pick the employee who served you from a scrollable gallery, complete with their photo, name, and tag number.
- **Smooth Slider Interface:** Use prominent navigation arrows to slide through the employee list, ensuring you can easily find the right person without obstruction.
- **Clear Visuals:** Employee images and names are displayed clearly, with a bold "Select Employee" title to guide you.

### 3. Share Detailed Feedback

- **Notes Section:** Add optional notes to provide specific feedback about your experience, such as compliments or suggestions.
- **Easy Input:** Use a simple text field to type your thoughts, with a streamlined submission process.
- **Phone Number Extraction:** If you include a phone number in your notes, Rank Riser automatically detects it for follow-up purposes.

### 4. Offline Support

- **Seamless Offline Mode:** Rate experiences even without an internet connection, with data cached locally and synced when you're back online.
- **Connectivity Indicator:** A red or blue dot in the header shows whether you're in offline (red) or online (blue) mode, keeping you informed.

### 5. Personalized and Accessible Interface

- **Theme Toggling:** Switch between light and dark modes for comfortable use in any lighting condition.
- **Language Support:** Toggle between languages (e.g., English and Arabic) to make the app accessible to a wider audience.
- **Responsive Design:** Enjoy a smooth experience on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices, thanks to the fast performance of Vite.

### 6. Success Feedback

- **Confirmation Dialog:** After submitting your rating, see a friendly confirmation with a heart icon and a "Thank You" message.
- **Quick Reset:** The app clears your selections after submission, making it easy to submit another rating if needed.

## Getting Started

1. **Visit Rank Riser:** Open the app in your web browser on any device.
2. **Rate Your Experience:**
   - Select an emoji to express your satisfaction.
   - Choose the employee who assisted you from the slider.
   - Add optional notes to share more details.
3. **Submit Your Feedback:** Click the "Send" button to share your rating, even offline.
4. **Explore Settings:** Toggle themes or languages using the icons in the header for a personalized experience.

## Why Rank Riser?

Rank Riser empowers customers to share their experiences quickly and intuitively, helping businesses improve service quality. With its emoji-based ratings, employee selection, and offline capabilities, Rank Riser makes feedback fun, accessible, and meaningful.
